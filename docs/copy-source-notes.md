## Internal rationale preserved from the prior prototype copy

- Remove stakeholder-facing phrases such as "prototype," "stakeholder," "the site should," and other presentation notes from rendered UI.
- Keep the current early-stage truth visible:
  - The project is still being built.
  - The Access to Care Lifeline is not yet fully operational.
  - Applications are not yet open.
- Remove unsupported or non-live claims from rendered copy:
  - The $40,000 launch target.
  - Founding-tier ladder copy and dollar amounts.
  - Speculative roadmap/reporting language that was not present on the live site.
- Keep current route structure even where the live IA differs, but rewrite those pages in the same public-facing voice.
- Preserve internal explanation in docs instead of in the rendered experience.
